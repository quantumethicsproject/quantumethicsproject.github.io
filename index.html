Quantum Ethics Project is a research project that aims to understand the quantum mechanics of quantum ethics.

  • Education

    Join the Quantum Ethics Project as we develop first-of-its-kind Coursework on Quantum Ethics. Whether you are a professional researcher, policy expert, or quantum novice, we are developing content to help you (Join the Conversation) on the social implications of quantum technology.

    (Get Involved) and connect with collaborators from our growing network of international researchers conducting fundamental research on quantum ethics, policy, economics, and more!
